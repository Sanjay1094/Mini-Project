Welcome To Tic-Tac-Toe(XO) Game

Rules for Tic-Tac-Toe(XO) :

=>You are X.
=>Your Friend (or) the Computer in this case is O.
=>Players Take Turns Putting their Marks in Empty Squares.
=>The First player to Get 3 of her Marks in a row(UP , DOWN , ACROSS , DIAGONALLY) in the Winner.
=>When all 9 squares are Full , The Game is Over....!
